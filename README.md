<h2 id="project">Description</h2> 

A simple, elegant tool to consolidate all your important links one stylish, shareable page, perfect for social media profiles.

BioLinks was designed to help users aggregate and organize their digital presence.

Key Features:
<ul>
    <li>Create and manage your own list of links.</li>
    <li>Personalize your page with your photo and more.</li>
    <li>Share your unique page handler with the entire web.</li>
</ul>
